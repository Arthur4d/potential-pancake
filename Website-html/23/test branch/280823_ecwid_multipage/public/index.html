Who Are We?

We are a purely family-run Vineyard, found in Kent in the South-East of England, offering tours and tastings upon request.

Vines

We planted 14,750 vines in 2020 and had our first harvest in 2022.

Varieties

We have 5 different varieties of grape; Bacchus, Pinot Blanc, Cabernet Noir, Reichensteiner and Seyval Blanc.

Passion

We run everything as a family, from taking care of the vines, harvesting and even creating this website!

Tours

Tours and tastings are available by request, get in touch and we can organise your visit!
